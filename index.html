About Me

Here is a gallery of some of my works

This was a Photoshop assignment that
I did last semester. For this project I
used the spiral focus effect. I focused
the radius around the bird so that the bird would stand out and the background would be blurred.

This is one of the first gifs I made
from taking multiple pictures from
the web and putting them together.
The most difficult part of making this
was trying to get the bees to enter
from different sides and angles of the
screen and individually moving each
bee towards the center where the
flowers were, while trying to make
the bees look like they were flying
smoothly.

This was the first flash animation that
I made using sound. I think the most
complicated part was making the
letter "A" fly towards the screen
and deciding how to make the other
letters spin and move off stage.

This is a gif of a snowboader. The
hardest part about making this gif
was trying to make the flip look
smooth and kind of realistic. Another
struggle was the sound. One of the
sounds kept playing over the gif when
it stopped, and figuring out why was
frustrating because I havent really
worked with sound before.

This was a gif I made using a sprite
sheet I found online. I chose a pig
sprite sheet because as I was looking
through the different types of sprite
sheets online, this one really stood
out to me because I thought that the
pig was really cute.

This is a gif animation I made of the
band duo Twenty One Pilots. The
strobe flashes were inspired by one of
their songs, called "Migraine". The
flashes do not exactly match the
beat of the song because I tried to
make the flashes consistent.

This was the first gif that I made
using flash animation. I thought this
project was an easy way to learn some
of the basic components of flash and
to get used to the new format and
tools of the program.

This is the first gif I made with a
consistent moving background. The
hardest part about this project was
trying to make the background flow
smoothly and adjusting the sounds.
